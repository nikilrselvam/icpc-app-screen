## ICPC Questions

#### Why ACM ICPC?
>I'm a math major and I love problem solving. I joined ACM not because of its software development offerings but because I wanted to think through tough problems and make friends with people who enjoy solving abstract problems like I do. That's what made ICPC appealing for me and I hope that through this program I'll get to engage with ICPC more frequently!

#### What’s your favorite problem? It does not have to be an algorithm, or even CS related!

#### Do you have any past experience with teaching? (It is not required that you do!)

I also taught some Vedic mathematics at my local ISKCON temple and have had long-term tutoring jobs working with younger children.
