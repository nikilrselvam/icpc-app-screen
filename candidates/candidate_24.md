I am a really competitive person so finding a community that fosters my competitiveness and helps turn that competitiveness into something positive is a community that I feel would greatly benefit me.

#### What’s your favorite problem? It does not have to be an algorithm, or even CS related!
>The nine dots, four lines problem (https://www.cut-the-knot.org/ghint.shtml) is my favorite problem. Basically you have a three by three table of dots and you have to use four lines to connect those dots with the lines being connected to each other. This is my favorite problem because it taught me to figuratively and literally think outside the box. After seeing the solution to this problem, I learned how to ignore the constraints that I implicitly gave myself.
