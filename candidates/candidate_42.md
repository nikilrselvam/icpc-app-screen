#### What’s your favorite problem? It does not have to be an algorithm, or even CS related!
>I’d say my favorite problem, or at least the one I’ve spent the most time thinking about, is how to win minimax. Minimax is a game where you have some tree with an arbitrary depth and an arbitrary number of branches at each node. Every node at the bottom has some value assigned to it. There are two players, each taking a turn by choosing a branch down the game tree to go. The goal of one player is to reach the highest value they can, and the goal of the other player is to reach the lowest value they can.
