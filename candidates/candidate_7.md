## ICPC Questions

#### Why ACM ICPC?
>While I haven't officially done a whole lot of competitive programming I was extremely involved in competition math during high school, and from the experience I do have with competitive programming, the basic ideas are the same: you get to know the subject(s), and you gain a level of understanding of these topics that is so fundamental and so basic that (and this is the coolest part) you start to see connections between them and other things that you never would have previously thought to ever be true, and you start to see them pop up, almost obviously like a gut feeling, in what might have before seemed like the strangest places --- i.e. some of those super-random seeming competition problems. I just love the feeling of seeing and making those connections and taking something that seems so complicated and strange and following it through to those clean, awesome solutions that competition problems often tend to have (dirtier/more complicated solutions are cool too; it just feels great to solve these problems in general)
